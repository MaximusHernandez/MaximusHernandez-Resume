My name is Maximus Alexander Hernandez, my roles that I play in my family are being a son, brother, leader, and student. I have been blessed with being athletic and having really good hand eye coordination which allows me to excel in almost every sport that I play. In high school I was a tri-scholar athlete, who played football, basketball, and baseball. These three sports have taken an enormous amount of time and effort throughout my life, however just recently that has stopped since now I am in college. Although, sports have played a large role in my life growing up, it was not necessarily the only thing that I was good at. I was also a good hardworking student as well who was smart to begin with. I have always had high grades in school and have tried my best to maintain them throughout the years. So, on top of being athletic, I put my academics above everything, because I have goals and dreams in life that I want to accomplish. One of my goals is to attend medical school, so I could eventually become a cardiovascular surgeon. I often wonder if this is the set career that I should take an interest to, however when looking upon the reason as to why I want this the answer seems clear to me. I’ve known what I have wanted to do ever since I was a kid. Due to losing my best friend to a heart disease, my passion and inspiration of becoming someone that I looked up to grew and I knew this was my calling.
